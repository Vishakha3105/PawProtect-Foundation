Our Story

Founded in 2018, PawProtect Foundation began with a simple belief: every animal deserves love, care, and a chance at happiness. What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a comprehensive animal welfare organization.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to rescue animals from dangerous situations, provide them with medical care, and find them loving forever homes. We believe in the power of community and education to create lasting change for animal welfare.

Through partnerships with local veterinarians, animal shelters, and compassionate individuals like you, we've been able to expand our reach and impact. Every rescue, every rehabilitation, and every successful adoption strengthens our commitment to our mission.
